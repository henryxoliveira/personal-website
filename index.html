About Me

I'm a junior at Santa Clara University, class of '27, studying Computer Science and Finance, with a minor in Mathematics.

Over the past few years, I've gained hands-on experience across startups, venture capital, and software development, most recently leading product development and GTM for my own startup, Parkd. I enjoy bridging the gap between engineering and business teams, whether that means coding, shaping product strategy, or driving customer adoption.

Outside of school and work, I'm usually playing guitar, staying active through fitness, or tending to a growing collection of plants. I love being outdoors and finding creative outlets like photography and chess that balance my analytical side. Looking ahead, I'm excited to keep growing as a product-minded engineer and future product manager, with a focus on solving problems that matter and working with teams to bring ideas to life.
