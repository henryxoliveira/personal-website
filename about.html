About Me

BIO

Background & Education

Santa Clara University

I'm a junior at Santa Clara University, class of '27, studying Computer Science and Finance, with a minor in Mathematics.

I am from São Paulo, Brazil, and moved with my family to the San Francisco Bay Area in 2015. Growing up in Silicon Valley while coming from Latin American immigrant household gave me a unique perspective: I've always been surrounded by innovation and technology, while also carrying the values of resilience, community, and adaptability that my family brought with us.

I'm passionate about the intersection of technology and business, particularly in product management and startup development.

Looking ahead, I'm excited to keep growing as a product-minded engineer and future product manager, with a focus on solving problems that matter and working with teams to bring ideas to life.
